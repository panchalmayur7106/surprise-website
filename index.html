Sometimes,
a simple moment of curiosity
changes the way a day feels.

I didn’t plan this like a speech,
or rehearse the words in my head.

I just let honesty
find a quiet place to exist.

You have this rare way
of making ordinary moments
feel unexpectedly gentle.

Like calm, without effort.

I don’t know when it started,
but somewhere between conversations
and silences,
you began to matter differently.

So before this day passes,
before courage changes its mind,
there’s something I wish to ask —
simply, sincerely.

This Valentine’s Day,
would you like to be
my life-long Valentine?

Take your time. This question isn’t in a hurry.
